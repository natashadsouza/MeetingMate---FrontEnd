ABOUT THE APP

Meetings form a vital part of each and every industry. Although essential, it is challenging and time consuming to keep a track of all the participants, the agenda, make notes and also track various decisions made during a single meeting. In an effort to optimize these manual processes that are in play during a meeting, we chose to work on an efficient Android application.
